Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how Ranked transforms your lead qualification process.

How does the AI scoring system work?

Our proprietary scoring algorithm evaluates each company using multiple data sources. We analyze industry, size, described problems, and online presence, then apply our specialized business intelligence models. The result is a comprehensive score from 0-100, with higher scores indicating better potential for AI consulting services.

What file formats are supported for upload?

Ranked currently supports Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV file formats. Your data should include company names, company size, and description fields for best results. Additional fields like industry and problems/needs will enhance the analysis.

How secure is my data?

We take data security seriously. All uploads are encrypted in transit and at rest. Our system processes your data using enterprise-grade security protocols and never shares your information with third parties. Processed files are automatically deleted after 24 hours.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our system combines multiple analytical approaches and has been thoroughly tested against expert evaluations. While no automated analysis is perfect, our proprietary system typically identifies high-value leads with 85-90% accuracy when compared to manual expert evaluation.

How long does it take to process my data?

Processing time depends on the number of companies in your dataset. Typically, analysis takes 5-10 seconds per company. A dataset of 100 companies would be processed in approximately 10-15 minutes.

Can I integrate Ranked with my CRM system?

Yes, we offer integration with popular CRM platforms including Salesforce, HubSpot, and Pipedrive. Our API allows for custom integrations with other systems. Contact us for specific integration requirements.

Do I need technical knowledge to use Ranked?

No technical knowledge is required. If you can upload an Excel file, you can use Ranked. The interface is designed to be intuitive, and the output is delivered in an easy-to-understand format with clear recommendations.

What's included in the pricing?

Our pricing is based on the number of leads processed per month. All plans include the core AI analysis, lead scoring, opportunity identification, and strategy recommendations. Enterprise plans include additional features like API access, custom integrations, and dedicated support.
